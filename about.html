Our Story

Every garment tells a unique story.

PRAAYA didn't begin as a business plan. It began as a belief — that clothing should be a reflection of one's soul, not just a size tag. That belief has driven every decision we've made since 2012, and it's why our clients keep returning for years, trusting us with their most precious occasions.

We've built our reputation on handcrafted precision. While mass fashion treats all designs the same, we've invested deeply in keeping traditional Indian craftsmanship alive. Our master karigars specialize in Aari and Zardosi embroidery, ensuring each detail is executed with historical techniques and modern refinement.

PRAAYA is women-owned and community-focused. We've always believed a design atelier should be a space of collaboration, where a client and designer work in harmony. Every visit is consultation-based, ensuring you get our full focus, dedicated styling advice, and a creation that is entirely yours.

What We Stand For

Three principles.
Non-negotiable.

01

Custom Artistry

We study fabrics, cuts, and colors before we stitch. Every client is unique. Our designers analyze your styling preferences and body measurements to draft a custom silhouette — not apply a standard pattern size and hope it works.

02

Handcrafted Excellence

We don't compromise on fabrics or details. Every material we source is premium silk, georgette, or velvet, and hand-embroidered by local artisans who display unmatched dedication to heritage stitches and modern detailing.

03

Consultation-First

Consultation-based only, because custom wear takes collaboration. You get a designer's undivided focus, detailed sketching, and a perfect fit trial. We don't rush. We don't cut corners. Everyone receives the same high standard of care.
